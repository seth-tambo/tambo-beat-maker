# Demo Scripts

Example prompts to quickly populate the beat canvas. Paste these into the chat to generate pads with pre-programmed patterns.

## Full Kit Setup

> Create a full drum kit with kick, snare, hi-hat, open hat, clap, rimshot, tom, and crash. Add interesting patterns to each one.

## Genre-Specific Beats

### Lo-fi hip hop

> Build me a lo-fi hip hop beat with 6 pads -- kick, snare, hi-hat, shaker, vinyl crackle, and a rim. Program chill patterns on all of them.

### House

> Set up 8 drum pads for a house beat: four-on-the-floor kick, offbeat hi-hats, snare on 2 and 4, clap, ride cymbal, tom fill, percussion, and a cowbell. Make it groovy.

## Trap

> Give me a trap beat -- kick, 808, snare, hi-hat with rolls, open hat, and a bell. Set the BPM to 140.

## Synth + Effects

> Play a synthwave arpeggio with filter sweep, reverb, and a driving beat.

## Tips

- Name specific drum sounds to control which pads get created.
- Ask for patterns on each pad to get pre-programmed sequences.
- Specify a genre or BPM to steer the style.
- The more instruments you name, the more pads appear on the canvas.
